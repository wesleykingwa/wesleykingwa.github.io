Microtransactions: Consumers Held Hostage

We are all tired of having to pay for extra content after having already paid for the game itself. This happens frequently today and often happens multiple times throughout the entirety of a given game.

The Racket

Some people think that you only have to purchase a video game to recieve the entirety of the content. But today that is not the case! Many have to pay extra multiple times while playing.

The Cost

The cost can vary depending on the type of game that you're playing. One thing is for sure, though. And that is that it is not cheap!

Artistic Limitations

Requiring the user to pay for certain content within the game can limit the creativity of the developer while creating the game.

Pay to Play

Implementing a "pay to play" system can turn a $60 game into a $100 game. It also, in a sense, gets the gamer hooked and makes purchasing the content more desireable
